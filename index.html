Log

July, 2018

Story

Arrived to Bali, Indonesia this week, where I will remain until I get my visa to live and work in Australia. Until that time I'm working remote from anywhere that is close to Perth, Australia and in the same timezone.

The past week was a regular work week for me. I set up shop at Hubud, a co-working space in the Balinese town of Ubud. My travelling colleague Tjin is also in Ubud.

Hubud has been a real efficiency booster for me. It's a really relaxing and stimulating place to work and the coffee is delicious. You have to look out for monkeys grabbing your stuff if you decide to work in the garden.

I'm sharing this adventure with Ana, my girlfriend, who is also working remote and enjoying Bali with me. On Saturday we did a night trekking to the summit of the volcanic Mt. Batur and on Sunday we hiked through the rice fields along the Campuhan Ridge. What a beautiful island!
